**"The Captain is a heavy-hitting zoner who can dampen the chaos of the battlefield, using Orbital Supply Beacons and Defensive Microbots to relieve himself and allies alike. Combining a well-timed Power Tazer shot with Orbital Probe strikes will devastate most crowds with ease, all while your trusty Vulcan Shotgun clears up whatever remains."**

Online compatibility not tested (Multiplayer disabled for now).

Features 2 original alt beacon skills and 6 alt skins, 3 unlockable (Judgement included!)

Also includes ModOptions (Check Ingame Settings -> Mods tab for configs!)
